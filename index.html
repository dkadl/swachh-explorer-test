About This Website

This platform follows the official Indian colour-coded dustbin system. Every colour is used throughout the design.

  • Green – Biodegradable/Organic
  • Blue – Dry/Recyclable
  • Red – Hazardous/Biomedical
  • Yellow – Sanitary/Medical
  • Black/Grey – General waste
